## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

FluentBee helps immigrants and workers learn the English vocabulary they actually need on the job — fast.

🐝 LEARN BY JOB SECTOR
Choose your work area — Construction, Hospitality, Healthcare, Agriculture, Automotive, and more. Every lesson is built around the real words and phrases used in that specific job.

📚 HOW IT WORKS
• Pick your job sector and role
• Learn vocabulary with images and audio
• Test yourself with Duolingo-style quizzes
• See your score and earn XP
• Level up through beginner to advanced

🎯 DUOLINGO-STYLE QUIZZES
See a word in your language → pick the correct English translation. Fast, visual, and effective.

🌍 AVAILABLE IN 12 LANGUAGES
The app works in English, Portuguese, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Dutch, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and more — so you can learn in your native language.

📈 TRACK YOUR PROGRESS
Earn XP points as you complete levels. Watch your progress grow as you master more vocabulary.

🔊 AUDIO PRONUNCIATION
Hear every word spoken clearly so you can say it right on the job — not just read it.

✅ WORKS OFFLINE
Downloaded lessons work without internet. Learn on the bus, on break, or anywhere.

WHO IS FLUENTBEE FOR?
• Immigrants starting a new job in an English-speaking country
• Workers who want to communicate better with colleagues and supervisors
• Anyone who needs practical, job-specific English — not textbook English
