# Privacy Policy — FluentBee

**Last updated: May 30, 2026**

FluentBee ("we", "our", or "us") operates the FluentBee mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Full name
- Email address
- Password (stored securely and never visible to us)

### Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- Learning progress (lessons completed, quiz scores, XP points)
- Language preferences
- App usage patterns (which job sectors and levels you study)

### Device Information
We may collect basic device information including device type and operating system version for app compatibility purposes.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:
- Create and manage your account
- Save and sync your learning progress across sessions
- Improve the app and its content
- Respond to your support requests

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We use industry-standard security measures including:
- Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure token storage on your device (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore)
- Row-level security ensuring you can only access your own data

---

The App uses the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |

| Supabase | Database and authentication | supabase.com/privacy |

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

FluentBee is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at support@fluentbee.app.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Export your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@fluentbee.app.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

---

## 9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

**Email:** support@fluentbee.app
